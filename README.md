# Vocal: Web Vocal Processor

A real-time, browser-based vocal effects processor built with the Web Audio API.

## Features

- **Input Gain**: Control the level of your microphone input.
- **Autotune**: Real-time pitch correction with adjustable speed and mix.
- **3-Band EQ**: Shape your sound with Low, Mid, and High frequency controls.
- **Compressor**: Smooth out dynamics with threshold, ratio, and makeup gain.
- **Chorus**: Add depth and width to your vocals.
- **Delay**: Parallel delay with adjustable time, feedback, and mix.
- **Reverb**: Convolution-based reverb for spatial enhancement.
- **Output Gain**: Final master volume control.

## How to Use

1.  **Hardware**: Use a pair of **headphones** to prevent audio feedback from your speakers into the microphone.
2.  **Open**: Launch `index.html` in a modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Brave).
3.  **Start**: Click the **Start** button in the top bar.
4.  **Permissions**: Grant the browser permission to access your microphone.
5.  **Adjust**: Use the sliders to shape your vocal sound in real time.
